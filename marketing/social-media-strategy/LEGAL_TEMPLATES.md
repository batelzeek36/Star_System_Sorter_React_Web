## How to Use These Templates

1. **Copy the template** you need
2. **Fill in the blanks** (your name, contributor name, dates, amounts)
3. **Have a lawyer review** (especially for equity and NDA)
4. **Send to contributor** via DocuSign, HelloSign, or email
5. **Get signature** before granting access
6. **Store signed copy** in a secure folder (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)

## State-Specific Notes

### California
- Non-compete clauses are generally unenforceable
- Use "non-solicitation" instead (can't poach team members)
- Consult a CA lawyer for equity agreements

### New York
- Non-compete clauses are enforceable if "reasonable"
- Must be limited in time (1-3 years) and scope
- Consult a NY lawyer for specifics

### Other States
- Laws vary widely
- Always consult a local lawyer
- Better safe than sorry

## When to Hire a Lawyer

**You can probably DIY:**
- CLA for small contributors
- NDA-Lite for frontend contributors
- Bounty agreements under $500

**You should hire a lawyer:**
- Full NDA for senior contributors
- Equity agreements (always)
- IP assignment agreements (always)
- If you're incorporating (LLC, C-corp)
- If you're fundraising
- If someone breaches and you need to sue

**Cost estimates:**
- Template review: $200-500
- Custom agreement: $500-2,000
- Incorporation: $1,000-3,000
- Lawsuit: $5,000-50,000+ (avoid if possible)
