## AI Image Tools (For Stills & Thumbnails)

### Midjourney (Best Overall)
**What it does**: Text-to-image, highest quality  
**Best for**: Thumbnails, concept art, star system crests  
**Access**: Discord bot (paid subscription)

**Prompt Examples**:
```
/imagine cosmic starfield with Pleiades constellation, lavender and purple 
nebula, sacred geometry overlay, dark background, mystical atmosphere, 
cinematic lighting --ar 16:9 --v 6

/imagine ancient I Ching hexagram carved in stone, glowing with inner light, 
surrounded by cosmic energy, dark mystical background, photorealistic --ar 1:1 --v 6

/imagine Human Design bodygraph chart made of light and energy, floating in 
space, centers glowing, channels connecting, cosmic background, ethereal --ar 9:16 --v 6

/imagine Egyptian hieroglyphs transforming into DNA double helix, sacred 
geometry, cosmic background, lavender and gold, mystical lighting --ar 16:9 --v 6
```

**Style Modifiers to Add**:
- `--style raw` (more photorealistic)
- `--chaos 20` (more variation)
- `--ar 9:16` (vertical for Reels/TikTok)
- `--ar 16:9` (horizontal for YouTube)

### DALL-E 3 (via ChatGPT Plus)
**What it does**: Text-to-image, great for specific concepts  
**Best for**: Diagrams, educational visuals, clean graphics  
**Access**: ChatGPT Plus subscription

**Prompt Examples**:
```
"Create a diagram showing the I Ching hexagram structure: 6 horizontal lines, 
some solid (yang) some broken (yin), labeled 'Line 1' through 'Line 6', 
dark background, lavender accents, clean modern design"

"Visualize the 8 star systems as constellation symbols arranged in a circle: 
Pleiades, Sirius, Lyra, Andromeda, Orion Light, Orion Dark, Arcturus, Draco. 
Dark cosmic background, each constellation glowing in a different color"

"Show a Human Design bodygraph with 9 centers (head, ajna, throat, g, ego, 
sacral, solar plexus, spleen, root) connected by channels, cosmic style, 
lavender and purple gradients"
```

### Leonardo.ai (Best for Consistency)
**What it does**: Text-to-image with style consistency  
**Best for**: Creating a consistent visual brand  
**Access**: leonardo.ai (free tier available)

**Use Case**: Generate all 8 star system crests in the same style

## Quick Workflow (30-Min Video)

1. **Script** (5 min): Write what you'll say
2. **Visuals** (10 min): Generate 3-5 AI images/videos
3. **Record** (5 min): Film yourself or record voiceover
4. **Edit** (10 min): CapCut - cut together visuals + audio
5. **Export** (2 min): 1080p, 30fps, upload
