# Short-Form Content Strategy (TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts)

## Why Short-Form Matters

Long streams are for the converted. Short-form is for the discovery phase. You need 30-60 second pulses that:
- Establish your archetype (mythic builder, not guru)
- Tease the depth without revealing the method
- Create curiosity loops that drive people to Discord/streams

## Visual Style Guide

### Aesthetic
- Dark backgrounds (cosmic, starfield, deep purple/black)
- Gate symbols overlaid on visuals
- Your face in corner or full-screen (authentic, not polished)
- Text overlays for key phrases
- End cards with clear CTA

### Tone
- Confident but not arrogant
- Mythic but not mystical
- Technical but not dry
- Accessible but not dumbed down

### Music
- Ambient/cinematic (think Interstellar, Arrival)
- No lyrics (your voice is the focus)
- Low volume (don't overpower your narration)

## Metrics to Track

- **View-through rate** (how many people watch to the end)

## Red Lines (Don't Cross These)

1. **Don't reveal the mappings** - Even in a "behind the scenes" clip
2. **Don't overpromise** - "Accurate" yes, "will change your life" no
3. **Don't bash other systems** - Stay collaborative, not competitive
4. **Don't use clickbait** - Your hooks should be true, not exaggerated
5. **Don't burn out** - 1 video per day is plenty, don't force it

---

## When to Pivot

### If views are low but engagement is high:
- You're finding your people, keep going
- Double down on what's working
- Ask your audience what they want to see

### If views are high but engagement is low:
- Your hooks are working but content isn't landing
- Add more CTAs (ask questions, invite comments)
- Make it more interactive (polls, Q&A)

### If nothing is working:
- Try a different platform (maybe your audience is on Twitter, not TikTok)
- Change your hook style (less technical, more mythic)
- Show more of yourself (people connect with faces, not just concepts)
