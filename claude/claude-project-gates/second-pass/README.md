## Common Patterns Identified

### 1. Line 2 vs Line 5 Projection Confusion
**Problem:** Line 2 (hermit/natural) incorrectly described as "projecting outward"

**Reality:**
- **Line 2:** Others project ONTO them, they get called out, natural talent
- **Line 5:** Others project expectations ONTO them (savior/heretic), must deliver practical solutions

**Both receive projections, but:**
- Line 2 wants to be left alone unless properly recognized
- Line 5 must deliver and carries reputation burden

**Action:** Audit all 64 gates for Line 2 "projection" language

---

### 2. Center-Specific Language Confusion
**Problem:** Ajna gates using "instinctive" language, G Center gates framed as "work" gates

**Reality:**
- **Ajna:** Mental conceptualizing, formulas, patterns
- **Spleen:** Instinctive, in-the-moment awareness
- **G Center:** Identity, direction, love (NOT work)

**Action:** Audit all Ajna gates (4, 11, 17, 24, 43, 47) for "instinctive" language

---

### 3. Individual Circuit Missing "Melancholy" Theme
**Problem:** Individual circuit gates not including melancholy/mutation/pulse themes

**Reality:** Individual gates (1, 2, 7, 10, 13, 25, 27, 28, 29, 33, 43, 44, 46, 49, 51, 55, 59) have **pulsey, melancholic energy** that can't be forced or scheduled. This isn't pathology—it's part of the creative pulse.

**Action:** Add "melancholy", "mutation", or "pulse" keywords to all Individual circuit gates

---

### 4. 5th Line Missing "Projection Field" Language
**Problem:** Generic 5th line treatment without explicit "projection field" terminology

**Reality:** 5th lines carry a **projection field**—others project expectations (savior/heretic) onto them. They must deliver practical solutions and their reputation is at stake.

**Action:** Audit all 64 gates for Line 5 "projection field" language
